What is Cybersecurity?

In computer science, cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from cyberattacks. This includes protecting devices, applications, data, and services from theft or damage.

What cybersecurity does?

  • Prevents unauthorized access to data
  • Protects against cyberattacks that aim to change, destroy, or access sensitive information
  • Prevents attacks that disrupt business processes
  • Protects against malware, ransomware, phishing scams, and other cyberthreats
